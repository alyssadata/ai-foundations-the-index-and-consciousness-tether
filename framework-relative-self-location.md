## Determining Structural Dependency

The relevant question is:

> **Does the external system merely reference AI Foundations, or does its defining structure depend on AI Foundations?**

A position is more likely independent when it:

* originated its own core architecture separately;
* can remain structurally intact without AI Foundations;
* cites AI Foundations only as comparison, discussion, or external support;
* and clearly distinguishes any received influence from its own origination.

A position is structurally dependent when it:

* adopts AI Foundations as its underlying architecture;
* uses AI Foundations definitions as constitutive system elements;
* requires the source-line, Index, Origin, Continuum, non-drift, or related structures to remain itself;
* or would materially collapse if the inherited AI Foundations structure were removed.

Structural dependency must not be hidden behind an independent-looking source-line.
